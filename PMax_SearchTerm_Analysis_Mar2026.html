🔑 The Smoking Gun: Why This Is a Campaign Failure, Not Market Decline

The data reveals a pattern that makes organic demand decline statistically impossible as the explanation. Brand searches ("man matters"), competitor searches ("beardo"), generic category searches ("minoxidil"), and accessory searches ("derma roller") ALL dropped simultaneously in the same 7-day window. These four query types are driven by completely independent audience signals — they cannot all collapse organically at the same time. This is a supply-side algorithm failure.
